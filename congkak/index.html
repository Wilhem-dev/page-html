A traditional game of strategy and skill


What is Congkak?

Congkak is a traditional board game popular in Malaysia and other Southeast Asian countries. It is typically played by two players using a wooden board with small pits and marbles or seeds.

The goal is to collect as many marbles as possible in your own storehouse. The game requires strategy, memory, and anticipating your opponent's moves.
